Using CryptoHint to solve an example cryptogram
    
XM CPAM BVBAM PZ AMWGPKWFXFSFRL RDBK 
.. .... ..... .. .............. .... 
    
LPI BAM PZ LPIA AFQDRW. 
... ... .. .... ....... 
    
- IKBRRAFXIRMH
- ............

OK, this cryptogram has one obvious pattern: BVBAM could be EVERY, but CryptoHint finds 382 matches like EVENT, EVERY, AGAIN and AWAKE, all of which are common enough to be in a cryptogram. CryptoHint does find only one word for AMWGPKWFXFSFRL, RESPONSIBLITY, so let's use that first. 

    
XM CPAM BVBAM PZ AMWGPKWFXFSFRL RDBK 
BE .ORE ...RE O. RESPONSIBILITY T..N 
    
LPI BAM PZ LPIA AFQDRW. 
.O. .RE O. .O.R RI..TS. 
    
- IKBRRAFXIRMH
- .N.TTRIB.TE.

Looks good so far. BVBAM now has some letters filled in, so let CryptoHint do a search on it again. No need to retype BVBAM, if you had tried it earlier it will be in the list of recent cryptogram words so just tap its entry: this time only one word comes up, AWARE. 

    
XM CPAM BVBAM PZ AMWGPKWFXFSFRL RDBK 
BE .ORE AWARE O. RESPONSIBILITY T.AN 
    
LPI BAM PZ LPIA AFQDRW. 
.O. ARE O. .O.R RI..TS. 
    
- IKBRRAFXIRMH
- .NATTRIB.TE.

CryptoHint finds THAN and TUAN for RDBK. THAN is probably the correct choice, TUAN (a Malay form of respectful address for a man, equivalent to sir or mister; OR a carnivorous marsupial from Australia) is an obscure word.

    
XM CPAM BVBAM PZ AMWGPKWFXFSFRL RDBK 
BE .ORE AWARE O. RESPONSIBILITY THAN 
    
LPI BAM PZ LPIA AFQDRW. 
.O. ARE O. .O.R RI.HTS. 
    
- IKBRRAFXIRMH
- .NATTRIB.TE.

You can probably work out the rest. CPAM is probably MORE, PZ is probably OF...

    
XM CPAM BVBAM PZ AMWGPKWFXFSFRL RDBK 
BE MORE AWARE OF RESPONSIBILITY THAN 
    
LPI BAM PZ LPIA AFQDRW. 
.O. ARE OF .O.R RI.HTS. 
    
- IKBRRAFXIRMH
- .NATTRIB.TE.

The rest is obvious.

    
BE MORE AWARE OF RESPONSIBILITY THAN 
YOU ARE OF YOUR RIGHTS. 
- UNATTRIBUTED
