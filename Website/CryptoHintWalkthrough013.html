Cryptogram Walkthrough 013

2012.09.07

QZL JUH QHXS JUE CHUR QZL DUKXFP QVTLQZLH, UW XQ RUW XE QZL CUPW ALDVHL QLFLGXWXVE RZLE SUHLEQW UEC JZXFCHLE UJQOUFFP QUFNLC QV LUJZ VQZLH. - UECHLR Z. KUFJVFK

QZL appears three times, could be THE - and if so, QV is TO. L seems common, so could very well be E. Let's try both QZL=THE and QV=TO.

QZL JUH QHXS JUE CHUR QZL DUKXFP QVTLQZLH, UW XQ RUW XE QZL CUPW ALDVHL QLFLGXWXVE RZLE SUHLEQW UEC JZXFCHLE UJQOUFFP QUFNLC QV LUJZ VQZLH. - UECHLR Z. KUFJVFK
THE *** T*** *** **** THE ****** TO*ETHE*, ** *T *** ** THE **** *E*O*E TE*E****O* *HE* ***E*T* *** *H****E* **T***** T***E* TO E**H OTHE*. - ****E* H. ****O**

Great! QVTLQZLH=TOGETHER

QZL JUH QHXS JUE CHUR QZL DUKXFP QVTLQZLH, UW XQ RUW XE QZL CUPW ALDVHL QLFLGXWXVE RZLE SUHLEQW UEC JZXFCHLE UJQOUFFP QUFNLC QV LUJZ VQZLH. - UECHLR Z. KUFJVFK
THE **R TR** *** *R** THE ****** TOGETHER, ** *T *** ** THE **** *E*ORE TE*E****O* *HE* **RE*T* *** *H***RE* **T***** T***E* TO E**H OTHER. - ***RE* H. ****O**

ALDVHL=BEFORE, and LUJZ=EACH.

QZL JUH QHXS JUE CHUR QZL DUKXFP QVTLQZLH, UW XQ RUW XE QZL CUPW ALDVHL QLFLGXWXVE RZLE SUHLEQW UEC JZXFCHLE UJQOUFFP QUFNLC QV LUJZ VQZLH. - UECHLR Z. KUFJVFK
THE CAR TR** CA* *RA* THE FA**** TOGETHER, A* *T *A* ** THE *A** BEFORE TE*E****O* *HE* *ARE*T* A** CH***RE* ACT*A*** TA**E* TO EACH OTHER. - A**RE* H. *A*CO**

"THE CAR something CAN something THE something (FAMILY?) TOGETHER, ..." Ah, TRIP - "THE CAR TRIP CAN something THE FAMILY TOGETHER, ..."

QZL JUH QHXS JUE CHUR QZL DUKXFP QVTLQZLH, UW XQ RUW XE QZL CUPW ALDVHL QLFLGXWXVE RZLE SUHLEQW UEC JZXFCHLE UJQOUFFP QUFNLC QV LUJZ VQZLH. - UECHLR Z. KUFJVFK
THE CAR TRIP CAN *RA* THE FAMILY TOGETHER, A* IT *A* IN THE *AY* BEFORE TELE*I*ION *HEN PARENT* AN* CHIL*REN ACT*ALLY TAL*E* TO EACH OTHER. - AN*RE* H. MALCOLM

The author's name is ANDREW H. MALCOLM. Also, "... IN THE DAYS BEFORE TELEVISION WHEN PARENTS AND CHILDREN ACTUALLY TALKED TO EACH OTHER."

THE CAR TRIP CAN DRAW THE FAMILY TOGETHER, AS IT WAS IN THE DAYS BEFORE TELEVISION WHEN PARENTS AND CHILDREN ACTUALLY TALKED TO EACH OTHER. - ANDREW H. MALCOLM

Done!

Andrew H. Malcolm: born 1943; 26 years on the New York Times staff; served on the Los Angeles Times editorial staff; spokesperson and press secretary for Laura Bush, 1999-2001; author of several books.
