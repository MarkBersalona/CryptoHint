Cryptogram Walkthrough 015

2012.09.17

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB

R is either A or I, let's start with R=A.

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB
*** A **** ****** ****A**** ***A*, A ** *****A* A ***** *** **** *A*** ******* ****. - **** *****

VSSO - I'm guessing the -SS- pair is either -EE- or -OO-. JZGS ends in S, so let's try S=E.

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB
*** A *EE* ****E* **E*A*E** ***A*, A ** *E***A* A ***** *** ***E *A*E* *****E* *E**. - **** *****

I'm beginning to have second thoughts on R=A. After the comma, A would be followed by a two-letter word, and right now I can't think of what would fit after A: A OF; A TO; A IN; A DO; nothing seems to work. If R=I, however, I AM could fit. Let's try R=I.

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB
*** I *EE* ****E* **E*I*E** ***I*, I ** *E***I* I ***** *** ***E *I*E* *****E* *E**. - **** *****

So let's try ZP=AM next...

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB
*A* I *EE* ****E* **E*I*E** A*AI*, I AM *E**AI* I ***** *** *A*E *I*E* A****E* *EA*. - **** A*AM*

Maybe VSSO=KEEP? "Blank I KEEP blank..."

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB
*A* I KEEP ****EP **E*I*EP* A*AIP, I AM *E**AIP I ***** P** *A*E *I*E* AP***E* *EA*. - ***P A*AM*

Too many words ending in -P, I don't think VSSO=KEEP. Back it out.

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB
*A* I *EE* ****E* **E*I*E** A*AI*, I AM *E**AI* I ***** *** *A*E *I*E* A****E* *EA*. - **** A*AM*

Mmmmmm... I'm stuck. OK, let me try CryptoHint. With R=I and S=E, for FCSBRASOY CryptoHint finds 6 matches. In Frequency sort: PRESIDENT; POETIZERS; PREDIGEST; PREDILECT; CLERIHEWS; UREDINEAL. Let's try PRESIDENT.

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB
*AD I *EEN ***SEN PRESIDENT A*AIN, I AM *ERTAIN I ****D N*T *A*E *I*ED AN*T*ER *EAR. - ***N ADAMS

OLY=NOT, that is, L=O.

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB
*AD I *EEN **OSEN PRESIDENT A*AIN, I AM *ERTAIN I *O**D NOT *A*E *I*ED ANOT*ER *EAR. - *O*N ADAMS

"HAD I BEEN CHOSEN PRESIDENT..."

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB
HAD I BEEN CHOSEN PRESIDENT A*AIN, I AM CERTAIN I CO**D NOT HA*E *I*ED ANOTHER *EAR. - *OHN ADAMS

Author is John Adams. "HAD I BEEN CHOSEN PRESIDENT AGAIN, I AM CERTAIN I COULD NOT HAVE something ANOTHER YEAR."

JZA R VSSO XJLBSO FCSBRASOY ZWZRO, R ZP XSCYZRO R XLNUA OLY JZGS URGSA ZOLYJSC TSZC. - ILJO ZAZPB
HAD I BEEN CHOSEN PRESIDENT AGAIN, I AM CERTAIN I COULD NOT HAVE LIVED ANOTHER YEAR. - JOHN ADAMS

Done!

John Adams was the second President of the United States, 1797-1801. His son, John Quincy Adams, was the sixth President.
