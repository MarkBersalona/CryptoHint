Cryptogram Walkthrough 009

2012.08.28

YTAK NG LXQJ XLKCTWXQN, NRK VXN ZCJTQP ZG WGQNZCMWZ X YTHK ZRXZ LXOK NKQNK HCGL ZRTQPN NRK HGMQO TQ PTHZ NRGSN. - AMCZ EGQQKPMZ

X is probably A. ZRXZ could be THAT. Let's try both.

YTAK NG LXQJ XLKCTWXQN, NRK VXN ZCJTQP ZG WGQNZCMWZ X YTHK ZRXZ LXOK NKQNK HCGL ZRTQPN NRK HGMQO TQ PTHZ NRGSN. - AMCZ EGQQKPMZ
**** ** *A** A*****A**, *H* *A* T***** T* ****T***T A **** THAT *A** ***** **** TH**** *H* ***** ** ***T *H***. - ***T *******T

Not bad. NRK appears twice in the cryptogram - WHO might fit, but so could something like SHE. ZRTQPN - THOUGH? no, H only appears once. Could NG be IS? "Blank is blank blank," - worth a shot.

YTAK NG LXQJ XLKCTWXQN, NRK VXN ZCJTQP ZG WGQNZCMWZ X YTHK ZRXZ LXOK NKQNK HCGL ZRTQPN NRK HGMQO TQ PTHZ NRGSN. - AMCZ EGQQKPMZ
**** IS *A** A*****A*I, IH* *AI T***** TS *S*IT***T A **** THAT *A** I**I* **S* TH***I IH* *S*** ** ***T IHS*I. - ***T *S*****T

Nope, back it out. Note though that first letter of NRK is also first letter of NG, could be a useful clue.

YTAK NG LXQJ XLKCTWXQN, NRK VXN ZCJTQP ZG WGQNZCMWZ X YTHK ZRXZ LXOK NKQNK HCGL ZRTQPN NRK HGMQO TQ PTHZ NRGSN. - AMCZ EGQQKPMZ
**** ** *A** A*****A**, *H* *A* T***** T* ****T***T A **** THAT *A** ***** **** TH**** *H* ***** ** ***T *H***. - ***T *******T

NRGSN also starts with the same letter. If NRK were WHO then NRGSN would be WH**H and NG would be W* - WE would fit for NG, but NRGSN would be WHE*H and I think this is a dead end. (UPDATE: A second look at this line of reasoning shows I was mistaken in thinking NRGSN had its second and fifth letters identical, instead of its first and fifth. No wonder this was a dead end!)

Oh, ZG must be TO.

YTAK NG LXQJ XLKCTWXQN, NRK VXN ZCJTQP ZG WGQNZCMWZ X YTHK ZRXZ LXOK NKQNK HCGL ZRTQPN NRK HGMQO TQ PTHZ NRGSN. - AMCZ EGQQKPMZ
**** *O *A** A*****A**, *H* *A* T***** TO *O**T***T A **** THAT *A** ***** **O* TH**** *H* *O*** ** ***T *HO**. - ***T *O*****T

Search for NG, keeping NRGSN in mind: BO; DO, GO, LO, NO, SO, YO. If NG were SO, NRGSN would be SHO*S: SHOES would fit; so would SHOPS, maybe others too. Let's try NG=SO.

YTAK NG LXQJ XLKCTWXQN, NRK VXN ZCJTQP ZG WGQNZCMWZ X YTHK ZRXZ LXOK NKQNK HCGL ZRTQPN NRK HGMQO TQ PTHZ NRGSN. - AMCZ EGQQKPMZ
**** SO *A** A*****A*S, SH* *AS T***** TO *O*ST***T A **** THAT *A** S**S* **O* TH***S SH* *O*** ** ***T SHO*S. - ***T *O*****T

NRK must be SHE.

YTAK NG LXQJ XLKCTWXQN, NRK VXN ZCJTQP ZG WGQNZCMWZ X YTHK ZRXZ LXOK NKQNK HCGL ZRTQPN NRK HGMQO TQ PTHZ NRGSN. - AMCZ EGQQKPMZ
***E SO *A** A*E***A*S, SHE *AS T***** TO *O*ST***T A ***E THAT *A*E SE*SE **O* TH***S SHE *O*** ** ***T SHO*S. - ***T *O**E**T

NKQNK could be SENSE.

YTAK NG LXQJ XLKCTWXQN, NRK VXN ZCJTQP ZG WGQNZCMWZ X YTHK ZRXZ LXOK NKQNK HCGL ZRTQPN NRK HGMQO TQ PTHZ NRGSN. - AMCZ EGQQKPMZ
***E SO *AN* A*E***ANS, SHE *AS T***N* TO *ONST***T A ***E THAT *A*E SENSE **O* TH*N*S SHE *O*N* *N ***T SHO*S. - ***T *ONNE**T

"Blank SO blank blank, SHE blank (WAS?) blank TO"... CONSTRUCT? "...A blank THAT"... MADE? Let's try CONSTRUCT.

YTAK NG LXQJ XLKCTWXQN, NRK VXN ZCJTQP ZG WGQNZCMWZ X YTHK ZRXZ LXOK NKQNK HCGL ZRTQPN NRK HGMQO TQ PTHZ NRGSN. - AMCZ EGQQKPMZ
***E SO *AN* A*ER*CANS, SHE *AS TR**N* TO CONSTRUCT A ***E THAT *A*E SENSE *RO* TH*N*S SHE *OUN* *N ***T SHO*S. - *URT *ONNE*UT
