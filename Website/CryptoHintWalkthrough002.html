Cryptogram Walkthrough 002

2011.06.29

UZSES'X M NVQQSESRTS OSUKSSR M BZVGAXABZL MRN M OIDBSE XUVTPSE. - TZMEGSX D. XTZIGH

Single letter word is A. One of the instances is preceded by a 3-letter word starting with the same letter - AND A or maybe ARE A would fit. I'll try A alone first.

UZSES'X M NVQQSESRTS OSUKSSR M BZVGAXABZL MRN M OIDBSE XUVTPSE. - TZMEGSX D. XTZIGH
.....'. A .......... ....... A .......... A.. A ...... ........ - ..A.... .. ......

UZSES might be THERE or WHERE. THERE'S A would fit and be suitable to start a sentence; WHERE'S A doesn't make as much sense. Let's go with THERE'S first.

UZSES'X M NVQQSESRTS OSUKSSR M BZVGAXABZL MRN M OIDBSE XUVTPSE. - TZMEGSX D. XTZIGH
THERE'S A ....ERE..E .ET.EE. A .H...S..H. A.. A ....ER ST...ER. - .HAR.ES .. S.H...

THERE'S A blank... BETWEEN would fit... THERE'S A blank BETWEEN A blank AND A blank. Sounds right.

UZSES'X M NVQQSESRTS OSUKSSR M BZVGAXABZL MRN M OIDBSE XUVTPSE. - TZMEGSX D. XTZIGH
THERE'S A D...EREN.E BETWEEN A .H...S..H. AND A B...ER ST...ER. - .HAR.ES .. S.H...

DIFFERENCE fits.

UZSES'X M NVQQSESRTS OSUKSSR M BZVGAXABZL MRN M OIDBSE XUVTPSE. - TZMEGSX D. XTZIGH
THERE'S A DIFFERENCE BETWEEN A .HI..S..H. AND A B...ER STIC.ER. - CHAR.ES .. SCH...

CHARLES SCHULZ - the Peanuts creator? What was his middle initial? M? F? Not F, that's already taken. Let me fill in what I do know...

UZSES'X M NVQQSESRTS OSUKSSR M BZVGAXABZL MRN M OIDBSE XUVTPSE. - TZMEGSX D. XTZIGH
THERE'S A DIFFERENCE BETWEEN A .HIL.S..H. AND A BU..ER STIC.ER. - CHARLES .. SCHULZ

BUMPER STICKER!

UZSES'X M NVQQSESRTS OSUKSSR M BZVGAXABZL MRN M OIDBSE XUVTPSE. - TZMEGSX D. XTZIGH
THERE'S A DIFFERENCE BETWEEN A PHIL.S.PH. AND A BUMPER STICKER. - CHARLES M. SCHULZ

I got lucky, the initial got filled in. Charles M. Schulz. Last word must be PHILOSOPHY.

THERE'S A DIFFERENCE BETWEEN A PHILOSOPHY AND A BUMPER STICKER. - CHARLES M. SCHULZ
