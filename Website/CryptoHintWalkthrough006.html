Cryptogram Walkthrough 006

2011.07.02

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ

This should be an easy one. Two single-letter words. K is probably I, the first two words are probably something like IF I, and the contraction is probably I'D or I'M. N=A. 'IF I yadda yadda yadda, I'D everything else.' looks right.

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ
IF I .AD .I... ..... .. .... D... A ...., I'D ....D .I. ..A....I.. .. A... - A..A.A. .I.....

'IF I HAD something something...' Z=H

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ
IF I HAD .I.H. H.... .. .H.. D... A ...., I'D ....D .I. .HA....I.. .. A... - A..AHA. .I.....

BVUU has a 2-letter ending. -LL or -SS are common. So V must be a vowel. BVUU could also be FREE, but '...A FREE,' doesn't sound right. A and I are already used. '...A WELL,' '...A BELL,' fit but don't make much sense yet. I'll try V=E for now.

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ
IF I HAD .I.H. H..E. .. .H.. D... A .E.., I'D ....D .I. .HAE...I.. .. A... - A.EAHA. .I.....

V=E doesn't work, or I'm wrong about something else. Maybe NIU is ANY? '...OF ANY.' or '...OR ANY.' might work. I'll back out V=E and let NIU=ANY.

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ
IF I HAD YI.H. H.... .. .H.. D... A ..YY, I'D ..Y.D .IN .HA..Y.I.. .. ANY. - A..AHA. .I.....

U=Y is wrong. And if I=N is right it only occurs twice. Back them both out.

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ
IF I HAD .I.H. H.... .. .H.. D... A ...., I'D ....D .I. .HA....I.. .. A... - A..AHA. .I.....

The third word, UKRZB, might end ing -IGHT. Going through the consonants, BIGHT, CIGHT, FIGHT, JIGHT, KIGHT, LIGHT. MIGHT, NIGHT, PIGHT, RIGHT, SIGHT, VIGHT, WIGHT, ZIGHT. 'IF I HAD FIGHT', 'IF I HAD LIGHT', 'IF I HAD MIGHT', 'IF I HAD NIGHT' - RIGHT, SIGHT would also fit. Plug in the -IGHT for now.

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ
IF I HAD .IGHT H.... T. .H.. D... A T..., I'D ....D .I. .HA....I.G .. A... - A..AHA. .I.....

BC must be TO.

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ
IF I HAD .IGHT HO... TO .HO. DO.. A T..., I'D ....D .I. .HA....I.G .. A... - A..AHA. .I..O..

Still no idea of the author's first name. Maybe LZNVGUJKJR ends in -ING, try J=N.

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ
IF I HAD .IGHT HO... TO .HO. DO.N A T..., I'D ...ND .I. .HA...NING .. A... - A..AHA. .IN.O.N

TREE! 'IF I HAD something something TO CHOP DOWN A TREE,'

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ
IF I HAD EIGHT HO.R. TO CHOP DOWN A TREE, I'D .PEND .I. .HARPENING .. A.E. - A.RAHA. .INCO.N

'IF I HAD EIGHT HOURS TO CHOP DOWN A TREE, I'D SPEND SIX SHARPENING MY AXE' - right?

KO K ZNE UKRZB ZCWVL BC YZCG ECMJ N BVUU, K'E LGUJE LKI LZNVGUJKJR AQ NIU. - NFVNZNA TKJYCTJ
IF I HAD EIGHT HOURS TO CHOP DOWN A TREE, I'D SPEND SIX SHARPENING MY AXE. - A.RAHAM .INCO.N

ABRAHAM LINCOLN.
