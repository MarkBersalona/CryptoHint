Cryptogram Walkthrough 012

2012.09.03

VQL VWJH UQ GQZEGQG SL BDXH HDQR BENN LSH GS, XLG HDQL HDQR XYQ XUNQ HS XZH BEHD IEKSY EL BDXH HDQR SWKDH HS GS. - VQLZEWJ

First word of the cryptogram might be THE, if so the second word starts with a T. BENN has two of the same letter at the end, and its second letter, probably a vowel, is not E - though FREE would fit the pattern also. Last two words are two-letter words, both ending in the same letter - TO DO would fit, NO GO would fit but probably wouldn't be the ending of a cryptogram. GQZEGQG has some repeating letters, though I don't recognize the pattern. HDQL HDQR - two words with the first 3 letters the same - what might fit? THEY THEN; EAST EASY; probably others.

Lots of Qs, Hs, maybe one of them is E? If H=E, HS would start with E, so that probably doesn't work. Let me try Q=E.

VQL VWJH UQ GQZEGQG SL BDXH HDQR BENN LSH GS, XLG HDQL HDQR XYQ XUNQ HS XZH BEHD IEKSY EL BDXH HDQR SWKDH HS GS. - VQLZEWJ
*E* **** *E *E***E* ** **** **E* **** *** **, *** **E* **E* **E ***E ** *** **** ***** ** **** **E* ***** ** **. - *E*****

UQ=*E - BE, HE, ME, WE, even YE could fit, come back to that later. Let's try BENN=WILL.

VQL VWJH UQ GQZEGQG SL BDXH HDQR BENN LSH GS, XLG HDQL HDQR XYQ XUNQ HS XZH BEHD IEKSY EL BDXH HDQR SWKDH HS GS. - VQLZEWJ
*E* **** *E *E*I*E* ** W*** **E* WILL *** **, *** **E* **E* **E **LE ** *** WI** *I*** I* W*** **E* ***** ** **. - *E**I**

Let's try BDHX=WHAT: "...WHAT blank WILL..."

VQL VWJH UQ GQZEGQG SL BDXH HDQR BENN LSH GS, XLG HDQL HDQR XYQ XUNQ HS XZH BEHD IEKSY EL BDXH HDQR SWKDH HS GS. - VQLZEWJ
*E* ***T *E *E*I*E* ** WHAT THE* WILL **T **, A** THE* THE* A*E A*LE T* A*T WITH *I*** I* WHAT THE* ***HT T* **. - *E**I**

Hey, looks pretty good! "...AND THEN THEY ARE ABLE TO something WITH..."

VQL VWJH UQ GQZEGQG SL BDXH HDQR BENN LSH GS, XLG HDQL HDQR XYQ XUNQ HS XZH BEHD IEKSY EL BDXH HDQR SWKDH HS GS. - VQLZEWJ
*EN ***T BE DE*IDED ON WHAT THEY WILL NOT DO, AND THEN THEY ARE ABLE TO A*T WITH *I*OR IN WHAT THEY O**HT TO DO. - *EN*I**

"MEN MUST BE DECIDED ON WHAT THEY WILL NOT DO,"

VQL VWJH UQ GQZEGQG SL BDXH HDQR BENN LSH GS, XLG HDQL HDQR XYQ XUNQ HS XZH BEHD IEKSY EL BDXH HDQR SWKDH HS GS. - VQLZEWJ
MEN MUST BE DECIDED ON WHAT THEY WILL NOT DO, AND THEN THEY ARE ABLE TO ACT WITH *I*OR IN WHAT THEY OU*HT TO DO. - MENCIUS

SWKDH=OUGHT, that is, K=G

VQL VWJH UQ GQZEGQG SL BDXH HDQR BENN LSH GS, XLG HDQL HDQR XYQ XUNQ HS XZH BEHD IEKSY EL BDXH HDQR SWKDH HS GS. - VQLZEWJ
MEN MUST BE DECIDED ON WHAT THEY WILL NOT DO, AND THEN THEY ARE ABLE TO ACT WITH *IGOR IN WHAT THEY OUGHT TO DO. - MENCIUS

RIGOR - no, first letter is NOT the same as the last. VIGOR!

MEN MUST BE DECIDED ON WHAT THEY WILL NOT DO, AND THEN THEY ARE ABLE TO ACT WITH VIGOR IN WHAT THEY OUGHT TO DO. - MENCIUS

"Mencius" sounded like a Roman name, but actually he was a Chinese Confucian philosopher, lived 372-289 B.C.
