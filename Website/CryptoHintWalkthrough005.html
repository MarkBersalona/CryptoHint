Cryptogram Walkthrough 005

2011.07.02

OT IPET UXK NXMK QPDKI, SBK OT IPET KAPUMQTAATH DK QAXZ FXH KX KIT ZTHDVPN GAXQTMMDXU. - FTXAFT STAUPAH MIPO

Not a lot of easy patterns here. OT occurs twice; KX KIT both start with the same letter, maybe TO THE or AS ANY might fit. There's a comma in the sentence, perhaps the first phrase starts with IF? 'IF blank blank something something, tla IF yadda...' dunno. K occurs a couple of times, but is also the last letter of both UXK and SBK - K is not E, so neither UXK nor SBK would be THE.

Shot in the dark - I'm going to try using TO THE for KX KIT and see what I get:

OT IPET UXK NXMK QPDKI, SBK OT IPET KAPUMQTAATH DK QAXZ FXH KX KIT ZTHDVPN GAXQTMMDXU. - FTXAFT STAUPAH MIPO
.E H..E .OT .O.T ...TH, ..T .E H..E T.....E..E. .T ..O. .O. TO THE .E..... ..O.E...O.. - .EO..E .E..... .H..

I think that works! Author's first name is GEORGE.

OT IPET UXK NXMK QPDKI, SBK OT IPET KAPUMQTAATH DK QAXZ FXH KX KIT ZTHDVPN GAXQTMMDXU. - FTXAFT STAUPAH MIPO
.E H..E .OT .O.T ...TH, ..T .E H..E TR....ERRE. .T .RO. GO. TO THE .E..... .RO.E...O.. - GEORGE .ER..R. .H..

'WE HAVE something something something' - maybe. 'GO. TO THE', what's the missing letter? GOB, GOC, GOD, GOF, GOJ, GOM, GON, GOP, GOS, GOV, GOW, GOX, GOY, GOZ. There's a 2-letter word ending with T: AT, IT, UT. Maybe H=D, as in '...TRANSFERRED IT something something TO THE' - I don't mean to say KAPUMQTAATH is TRANSFERRED, though it would fit, hmmmm. Try TRANSFERRED.

OT IPET UXK NXMK QPDKI, SBK OT IPET KAPUMQTAATH DK QAXZ FXH KX KIT ZTHDVPN GAXQTMMDXU. - FTXAFT STAUPAH MIPO
.E HA.E NOT .OST FA.TH, ..T .E HA.E TRANSFERRED .T FRO. GOD TO THE .ED..A. .ROFESS.ON. - GEORGE .ERNARD SHA.

'WE HAVE NOT LOST FAITH, BUT WE HAVE TRANSFERRED IT FROM GOD TO THE something PROFESSION. - GEORGE BERNARD SHAW'. Plug in everything I know and maybe the missing word will get filled in.

OT IPET UXK NXMK QPDKI, SBK OT IPET KAPUMQTAATH DK QAXZ FXH KX KIT ZTHDVPN GAXQTMMDXU. - FTXAFT STAUPAH MIPO
