Cryptogram Walkthrough 007

2011.07.06

NI QIO BQOLKLJBOG OFIDCZG, IF EIFFT BCIDO EMBO YBT QGHGF MBJJGQ. VGGJ LQ OMG ADQZLSMO. - CGQUBYLQ RFBQVZLQ

A few clues. VGGJ has two repeating letters in the middle, probably -EE- or -OO-. A few 2-letter and 3-letter words, do any of them have G? OMG has one. Let's try OMG=THE.

NI QIO BQOLKLJBOG OFIDCZG, IF EIFFT BCIDO EMBO YBT QGHGF MBJJGQ. VGGJ LQ OMG ADQZLSMO. - CGQUBYLQ RFBQVZLQ
.. ..T ..T.....TE T.....E, .. ..... ....T .H.T ... .E.E. H...E.. .EE. .. THE ......HT. - .E...... ........

EMBO could be WHAT. QGHGF could be NEVER. EMBO is WHAT, EIFFT would start with a W - WORRY could fit, for example. Let's try QGHGF as NEVER:

NI QIO BQOLKLJBOG OFIDCZG, IF EIFFT BCIDO EMBO YBT QGHGF MBJJGQ. VGGJ LQ OMG ADQZLSMO. - CGQUBYLQ RFBQVZLQ
.. N.T .NT.....TE TR....E, .R ..RR. ....T .H.T ... NEVER H...EN. .EE. .N THE ..N...HT. - .EN....N .R.N...N

'DO NOT something something,' sounds right, and EIFFT does look like it could be WORRY. Let's fill in for WHAT and DO NOT first.

NI QIO BQOLKLJBOG OFIDCZG, IF EIFFT BCIDO EMBO YBT QGHGF MBJJGQ. VGGJ LQ OMG ADQZLSMO. - CGQUBYLQ RFBQVZLQ
DO NOT ANT....ATE TRO...E, OR WORR. A.O.T WHAT .A. NEVER HA..EN. .EE. .N THE ..N...HT. - .EN.A..N .RAN...N

'DO NOT ANTICIPATE TROUBLE, OR WORRY ABOUT WHAT something NEVER HAPPEN.'

NI QIO BQOLKLJBOG OFIDCZG, IF EIFFT BCIDO EMBO YBT QGHGF MBJJGQ. VGGJ LQ OMG ADQZLSMO. - CGQUBYLQ RFBQVZLQ
DO NOT ANTICIPATE TROUBLE, OR WORRY ABOUT WHAT .AY NEVER HAPPEN. .EEP IN THE .UNLI.HT. - BEN.A.IN .RAN.LIN

The author is Benjamin Franklin.

NI QIO BQOLKLJBOG OFIDCZG, IF EIFFT BCIDO EMBO YBT QGHGF MBJJGQ. VGGJ LQ OMG ADQZLSMO. - CGQUBYLQ RFBQVZLQ
DO NOT ANTICIPATE TROUBLE, OR WORRY ABOUT WHAT MAY NEVER HAPPEN. KEEP IN THE .UNLI.HT. - BENJAMIN FRANKLIN

Missing word must be SUNLIGHT. Just to check, the remaining unused letters are G, Q, S, X, Z. Nope, can't see how any other letters work. SUNLIGHT it is.

DO NOT ANTICIPATE TROUBLE, OR WORRY ABOUT WHAT MAY NEVER HAPPEN. KEEP IN THE SUNLIGHT. - BENJAMIN FRANKLIN
