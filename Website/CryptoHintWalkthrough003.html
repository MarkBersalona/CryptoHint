Cryptogram Walkthrough 003

2011.07.01

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH

Well, let's start with the easy ones. There are two single-letter words. The first word must be I since the word in the cryptogram is A and in a typical cryptogram a letter cannot stand for itself. O would be very rare. So, let A=I and J=A.

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH
I ....... ..A. ... .... ..... ..A. I........ .. A.. ..... ... ..I.. .. A.. ..A.., A.. ..I.. .A.. .... A .I.... ....... .... ... .A..I...A. .A.. ..A. .. .A.. ... ... .......... - ...A.. .......

I don't see anything obvious right now... JBV might be AND - 'I blank blank yadda yadda yadda, AND blank blank...' could be a compound sentence. Don't have anything else to go on right now, so let's try AND.

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH
I ....... ..A. ... .N.. ..... ..A. IN....N.. .. A.. ..... ... ..I.. .. A.. ..AD., AND ..I.. .A.. ..N. A .I.... ....... D..N ... .A..I...A. .A.. ..AN .. .A.. ... ... .......... - .D.A.D .......

Maybe VTRB could be DOWN? ABOKFIBQI might be IN-something-ING - no, I've already used I and there's still another letter left at the end. Oh, DGJD could be THAT! Let's try...

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH
I ......T THAT TH. .N.. ..... THAT IN....N.. .. A.. TH... ... .HI.H .. A.. ..AD., AND .HI.H HA.. ..N. A .ITT.. ...TH.. D..N ... .A.TI...A. .ATH THAN .. HA.. ..T ..T .......... - .D.A.D ....T..

So DGI must be THE. OK, THY would fit too, but it's probably THE.

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH
I ....E.T THAT THE .N.. ..... THAT IN...EN.E .. A.E TH..E ... .HI.H .E A.E .EAD., AND .HI.H HA.E ..NE A .ITT.E ...THE. D..N ... .A.TI...A. .ATH THAN .E HA.E .ET ..T ....E..E.. - ED.A.D ....TE.

INDEPENDENCE? No, doesn't fit. AND WHICH HAVE?

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH
I ....E.T THAT THE .N.. ..... THAT IN...ENCE .. A.E TH..E ... WHICH WE A.E .EAD., AND WHICH HAVE ..NE A .ITT.E ...THE. D.WN ... .A.TIC..A. .ATH THAN WE HAVE .ET ..T ....E.VE.. - EDWA.D ....TE.

LITTLE. KADDKI is LITTLE, I should have recognized the pattern. And the author's first name is EDWARD.

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH

'I something THAT THE ONLY something THAT...' INSOLENCE? And PARTICULAR further along the sentence...

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH

Oops, INSOLENCE doesn't fit.

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH

'...ARE THOSE FOR WHICH WE ARE READY' works. Plug in for PARTICULAR also...

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH
I SU..EST THAT THE ONLY .OO.S THAT INFLUENCE US ARE THOSE FOR WHICH WE ARE READY, AND WHICH HAVE .ONE A LITTLE FURTHER DOWN OUR PARTICULAR PATH THAN WE HAVE YET .OT OURSELVES. - EDWARD FORSTER

SUGGEST.

A YFUUIYD DGJD DGI TBKZ NTTLY DGJD ABOKFIBQI FY JHI DGTYI OTH RGAQG RI JHI HIJVZ, JBV RGAQG GJPI UTBI J KADDKI OFHDGIH VTRB TFH SJHDAQFKJH SJDG DGJB RI GJPI ZID UTD TFHYIKPIY. - IVRJHV OTHYDIH
I SUGGEST THAT THE ONLY .OO.S THAT INFLUENCE US ARE THOSE FOR WHICH WE ARE READY, AND WHICH HAVE GONE A LITTLE FURTHER DOWN OUR PARTICULAR PATH THAN WE HAVE YET GOT OURSELVES. - EDWARD FORSTER

The remaining, unused letters are B, J, K, M, Q, X and Z. BOOJS - no. BOOKS - that's a word. 'I SUGGEST THAT THE ONLY BOOKS THAT INFLUENCE US' - yes, that's it! Could it be anything else? MOOKS - um, no. KOOBS, BOOZS, ZOOMS, MOOBS, trying a bunch of other letters, no I think BOOKS makes the most sense.

I SUGGEST THAT THE ONLY BOOKS THAT INFLUENCE US ARE THOSE FOR WHICH WE ARE READY, AND WHICH HAVE GONE A LITTLE FURTHER DOWN OUR PARTICULAR PATH THAN WE HAVE YET GOT OURSELVES. - EDWARD FORSTER

Who's Edward Forster? Wikipedia has a William Edward Forster, a British industrialist and Chief Secretary for Ireland in the 19th century. Wikipedia also has an E. M. Forster, or Edward Morgan Forster, a British novelist, 1879-1970. Ah, and doing a search for 'Edward Forster quotes' I've found Edward M. Forster said it.
