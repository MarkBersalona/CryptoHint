Cryptogram Walkthrough 001

2011.06.29

This is the first of what I hope will be a series of cryptogram walkthroughs. The purpose is to show a beginner how to solve a cryptogram by example, including the thought process, mistakes and dead ends, and any external help from tools like CryptoHint or web searches.

AX AJ UQFSJX UJ AFWSGXUBX XS RBSK KCUX AJ BSX JPGASVJ UJ XS RBSK KCUX AJ.

Ah, my favorite beginning of a cryptogram: IT IS is easily recognized because the two 2-letter words start with the same letter, and a declarative sentence can begin with IT IS.

AX AJ UQFSJX UJ AFWSGXUBX XS RBSK KCUX AJ BSX JPGASVJ UJ XS RBSK KCUX AJ.
IT IS ....ST .S I....T..T T. .... ...T IS ..T S..I..S .S T. .... ...T IS.

The 2-letter word beginning with T must be TO.

AX AJ UQFSJX UJ AFWSGXUBX XS RBSK KCUX AJ BSX JPGASVJ UJ XS RBSK KCUX AJ.
IT IS ...OST .S I..O.T..T TO ..O. ...T IS .OT S..IO.S .S TO ..O. ...T IS.

UJ must be AS, since I is already used. ES doesn't work, O is already used, US is a valid word, but IT IS blank AS blank sounds more likely than IT IS blank US blank. Let's assume AS.

AX AJ UQFSJX UJ AFWSGXUBX XS RBSK KCUX AJ BSX JPGASVJ UJ XS RBSK KCUX AJ.
IT IS A..OST AS I..O.TA.T TO ..O. ..AT IS .OT S..IO.S AS TO ..O. ..AT IS.

IT IS ALMOST AS IMPORTANT sounds right, let's try that.

AX AJ UQFSJX UJ AFWSGXUBX XS RBSK KCUX AJ BSX JPGASVJ UJ XS RBSK KCUX AJ.
IT IS ALMOST AS IMPORTANT TO .NO. ..AT IS NOT S.RIO.S AS TO .NO. ..AT IS.

... TO KNOW WHAT IS NOT blank AS TO KNOW WHAT IS sounds right.

AX AJ UQFSJX UJ AFWSGXUBX XS RBSK KCUX AJ BSX JPGASVJ UJ XS RBSK KCUX AJ.
IT IS ALMOST AS IMPORTANT TO KNOW WHAT IS NOT S.RIO.S AS TO KNOW WHAT IS.

The final word to be solved must be SERIOUS. Curious, this cryptogram only has a single E!

IT IS ALMOST AS IMPORTANT TO KNOW WHAT IS NOT SERIOUS AS TO KNOW WHAT IS.
