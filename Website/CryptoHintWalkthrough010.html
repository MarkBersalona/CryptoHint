Cryptogram Walkthrough 010

2012.08.29

EXIETX KJTT MI AJMQU ID EAXPXAAJDM UI NI ZWHJDXHH KJUQ PAJXDNH. - WDSUUAJZWUXN

EXIETX is probably PEOPLE. Finally I recognize the pattern!

EXIETX KJTT MI AJMQU ID EAXPXAAJDM UI NI ZWHJDXHH KJUQ PAJXDNH. - WDSUUAJZWUXN
PEOPLE **LL *O ***** O* P*E*E***** *O *O *****E** **** ***E***. - **********E*

"PEOPLE WILL DO..." or maybe "PEOPLE WILL GO..." - let's try WILL for now.

EXIETX KJTT MI AJMQU ID EAXPXAAJDM UI NI ZWHJDXHH KJUQ PAJXDNH. - WDSUUAJZWUXN
PEOPLE WILL *O *I*** O* P*E*E**I** *O *O ***I*E** WI** **IE***. - ******I***E*

I'm guessing MI is DO and ID is ON.

EXIETX KJTT MI AJMQU ID EAXPXAAJDM UI NI ZWHJDXHH KJUQ PAJXDNH. - WDSUUAJZWUXN
PEOPLE WILL DO *ID** ON P*E*E**IND *O *O ***INE** WI** **IEN**. - *N****I***E*

Hmmm, maybe MI is GO instead, then EAXPXAAJDM will end in -ING instead of -IND. Change MI to GO.

EXIETX KJTT MI AJMQU ID EAXPXAAJDM UI NI ZWHJDXHH KJUQ PAJXDNH. - WDSUUAJZWUXN
PEOPLE WILL GO *IG** ON P*E*E**ING *O *O ***INE** WI** **IEN**. - *N****I***E*

"PEOPLE WILL GO RIGHT ON something..."

EXIETX KJTT MI AJMQU ID EAXPXAAJDM UI NI ZWHJDXHH KJUQ PAJXDNH. - WDSUUAJZWUXN
PEOPLE WILL GO RIGHT ON PRE*ERRING TO *O ***INE** WITH *RIEN**. - *N*TTRI**TE*

PREFERRING; and the author is UNATTRIBUTED.

EXIETX KJTT MI AJMQU ID EAXPXAAJDM UI NI ZWHJDXHH KJUQ PAJXDNH. - WDSUUAJZWUXN
PEOPLE WILL GO RIGHT ON PREFERRING TO DO BU*INE** WITH FRIEND*. - UNATTRIBUTED

"...BUSINESS WITH FRIENDS."

PEOPLE WILL GO RIGHT ON PREFERRING TO DO BUSINESS WITH FRIENDS. - UNATTRIBUTED

I did a search on the quote, it seems to be a common business quote, often as one of a number of quotes in a "Business Forecast" quoted on websites of local businesses. During the search I found the "Business Forecast" on the websites of a car dealer and a restaurant. Could eventually be hackneyed, like "You've tried the rest, now try the best." Personally I pay more attention when it's the business owner's own words, the genuine personal touch, rather than something copied. As an unsponsored example, see Rivendell Bicycle Works at rivbike.com as an example of a business run with passion.
