Cryptogram Walkthrough 008

2011.07.06

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF

Short one. Last word has innermost 2 letters repeating, and next 2 letters repeating - could be a word like BETTER or LETTER. First word ZJY has a J in the middle, so could be E or O, though really haven't ruled anything out yet. Oh, AJPPJC is repeated, appears twice. COMMON would fit too. C only appears in these two words. Sometimes the shorter the cryptogram the harder it is to solve - fewer clues. I'm going to try -ETTER first.

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF
.E. T... .... .ETTER ..E..., ....... .. T... ...T .E .ETTER. - ...... .....

Nothing extraordinary, either for or against my first choice. '...BE BETTER.' doesn't fit, '...WE BETTER.' doesn't sound right except in very relaxed dialogue. I'll try COMMON instead.

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF
.O. M... .... COMMON ..O..., ..C.... .. M... ...M .O COMMON. - ...... .....

'YOU MUST something COMMON something,'?

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF
YOU M... .... COMMON ..O..., ..C.... .. M.U. ...M .O COMMON. - ...... .....

I was wrong, YOU MUST doesn't fit. Back it out. Or... 'YOU MAKE something COMMON something,'? Let's continue with YOU MAKE.

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF
YOU MAKE ..E. COMMON ..O..., ..C.AK. .. M.U. E..M KO COMMON. - ...... .....

No, doesn't work. Back out YOU MAKE.

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF
.O. M... .... COMMON ..O..., ..C.... .. M... ...M .O COMMON. - ...... .....

Second to last word is a 2-letter word, ending in -O if COMMON is right. 'DO COMMON', 'GO COMMON', 'SO COMMON', 'TO COMMON' - 'SO COMMON' sounds best, let's try G=S

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF
.O. M.S. .... COMMON ..O..., ..C..S. .. M... ...M SO COMMON. - ...... .....

'GOD MUST something COMMON something,' - OK, I can see God mentioned in a cryptogram. There's another 2-letter word, I'm guessing it starts with an I. Let me try GOD MUST first.

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF
GOD MUST ..T. COMMON ..O..., ..C.US. .. M.D. T..M SO COMMON. - ...... .....

'...MADE THEM SO COMMON.'?

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF
GOD MUST HATE COMMON .EO..E, .ECAUSE HE MADE THEM SO COMMON. - .H.... ....E

'...BECAUSE HE MADE THEM SO COMMON.' And NFJNHF is PEOPLE, I should have recognized the pattern. Hopefully the author's name gets filled in with enough clues.

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF
GOD MUST HATE COMMON PEOPLE, BECAUSE HE MADE THEM SO COMMON. - PH.L.P ..L.E

OK, the author's first name is PHILIP.

ZJY POGU TRUF AJPPJC NFJNHF, EFAROGF TF PRYF UTFP GJ AJPPJC. - NTVHVN BQHVF
GOD MUST HATE COMMON PEOPLE, BECAUSE HE MADE THEM SO COMMON. - PHILIP ..LIE

No clue on the author's last name. Any vowels left? No. Letters left in general: F, J, K, Q, R, V, W, X, Y, Z. So Y must be one of the remaining letters, probably Q=Y. WYLIE is a surname. Maybe KYLIE would fit too? RYLIE as a variant on the Irish RILEY?

Do a search on Philip Wylie. According to Wikipedia, Philip Gordon Wylie (1902-1971) was an American author, son of a Presbyterian minister. And doing a search on the quote itself identifies the author as Philip Wylie.

GOD MUST HATE COMMON PEOPLE, BECAUSE HE MADE THEM SO COMMON. - PHILIP WYLIE
